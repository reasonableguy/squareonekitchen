Ingredients

    (makes 1/2 a cup of dressing)

  • 1/4 cup buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup sourcream
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon chives
  • 1 tablespoon cilantro
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne

Method

  1. Finely chop the herbs.
  2. Combine all the ingredients in a jar slightly wider than your immersion blender.
  3. Place the immersion blender into the jar.
  4. Blend for 15-30 seconds.
  5. Store in the fridge.Shake vigourously to revitalize it before using
