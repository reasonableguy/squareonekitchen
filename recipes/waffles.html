Total time:

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1.5 tablespoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs (separated)
  • 1/2 cup canola oil
  • 2 cups milk

Method

  1. Turn your waffle maker on to preheat.
  2. Carefully separate your eggs. Place the whites in a mixing bowl and whisk until you get stiff peaks. (set the yolks aside for later).
  3. Combine all the dry ingredients in medium bowl.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine the yolks, milk and oil.
  5. When the wet ingredients are nicely combined, slowly add in the dry mixture.
  6. Whisk for a minute to form a good batter.
  7. Turn the mixer off and slowly flod in the egg whites.
  8. Lightly grease your waffle maker and add the batter according to the directions (ours takes half a cup of batter per waffle).
  9. Eat straight away or allow to cool on a cooling rack before storing in the freezer.
  10. They're easy to reheat from frozen in the toaster.
