Prep time:

Cook time:

Total time:

Ingredients

  • 1 sprig of fresh rosemary
  • 50g streaky bacon
  • olive oil
  • 1 kg quality minced beef
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 onion
  • 2 sticks of celery
  • 1 heaped tablespoons tomato purée
  • 2 x 400g tins of tomatoes
  • 800ml Water
  • 2 Tbsp Fish sauce
  • 1 Tbsp Soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp Unsweetened Cocoa

Method

  1. Strip and finely chop the rosemary and bacon.
  2. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large pot on a high heat. Once hot, add the rosemary and bacon and fry until the bacon starts to get crisy.
  3. Add all the ground beef. Break it up with a wooden spoon. Reduce the heat to medium and cook until all the liquid has evaporated, stirring occasionally.
  4. Roughly chop carrots, onions and celery. Add to the processor and blitz to roughly the same size as the beef.
  5. Add veggies to the pot. Cook for 15 to 20 minutes, or until the vegetables start to soften, stirring regularly.
  6. Add the tomato purée and tinned tomatoes. Fill each of the tins with water and tip into the pan.
  7. Add fish sauce, soy sauce and cocoa.
  8. Stir well and reduce the heat to low. Leave to simmer around an hour, or until thickened and reduced, stirring occasionally.
  9. Season to taste. Blitz in batches.
