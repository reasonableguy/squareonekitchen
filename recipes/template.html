Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 stalks celery, finely diced
  • 2 carrots, finely diced
  • 1lb ground beef
  • Â¼ cup tomato paste
  • 14oz can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 3 cups beef stock (or water)
  • 1 tsp fish sauce
  • Salt & Pepper

Directions

  1. Start by ctting your onion and garlic, and dicing your vegetables (you can also throw them in the food processor).
  2. Heat oil in a large pot on med-high heat. Add onion and cook until soft and starting to brown. Stir regularly. Then add the garlic and veggies and cook until softened.
  3. Remove the vegetables and put them to one side. Add in the beef and quickly brown.
  4. Add tomato paste and stir through. Then pour in the tomatoes to begin to deglaze the pot.
  5. Add the vegetables and aromatics back in, along with the stock and the herbs.
  6. Bring to the boil, then reduce to a simmer and cook uncovered for 45 minutes (you want the sauce to be thick so it sticks to your pasta).
  7. Add fish sauce and season to taste with salt and pepper. serve over your carbohydrate of choice (pasta, rice, toast...)
