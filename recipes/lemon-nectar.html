Prep time:

Cook time:

Total time:

Ingredients

  • 450g used lemons (about 6 lemons)
  • 230g sugar

Method

  1. Place the lemons in an airtight container with the sugar.
  2. Stir so that the sugar covers the lemons.
  3. Close the container.
  4. Leave it (at room temperature) for a couple of days.
  5. Stir it occasionally.
  6. When the sugar has dissolved completely, strain through a sieve.
  7. Enjoy in drinks or drizzled over tarts and cakes.
