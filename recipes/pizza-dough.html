Prep time:

Total time:

Ingredients

  • 150g 00 flour
  • 150g all-purpose flour
  • 8g fine sea salt
  • 2g active dry yeast
  • 4g extra-virgin olive oil

Method

  1. In a large bowl, combine flours and salt.
  2. In a small bowl, combine together 200g of lukewarm tap water, the yeast, and the olive oil.
  3. Pour the yeast mixture into the flour mixture.
  4. Knead with your hands until well combined (about 3-4 minutes). Then rest the dough for 15 minutes.
  5. Knead the rested dough for 3 more minutes. Then, cut in half and shape each piece into a ball.
  6. Place on a floured surface and cover with damp tea towel. Let rest for 3 to 4 hours (or overnight in the fridge.(Warm up to room temperature before using).
