Cook time:

Total time:

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 50g butter
  • 1 & 1/4 cup milk + extra for brushing

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the three dry ingredients.
  3. Cut the butter in until it ressembles breadcrumbs.
  4. Add 1 & 1/4 cups of milk and combine to a sticky dough.
  5. Pour the dough out onto a floured surface and knead lightly.
  6. Roll the dough out until about 1/4 inch thick.
  7. Fold the dough in half and cut the scones into your desired shape.
  8. Place the scones on a floured oven tray (with about an inch between the scones).
  9. Brush the tops with milk.
  10. Bake at 450°F for 10-15 minutes (until golden brown).
