Cook time:

Total time:

Ingredients

  • 1 meaty slab baby back ribs
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • ½ cup water (needed for the pressure cooker)
  • 1 cup BBQ Sauce

    • Barbecue Rub

  • ¾ tablespoon paprika
  • ¾ tablespoon brown sugar
  • ½ tablespoon chili powder
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ¼ teaspoon onion powder
  • ⅛ teaspoon dried thyme

Method

  1. Peel the membrane from the ribs. On the bone side of the ribs, use a butter knife, a spoon or your fingers and lift the silvery membrane away from the bone. When you have a corner up, use a paper towel for grip and pull the membrane off of the ribs.
  2. Make the rub: Mix together the barbecue rub ingredients.
  3. Season the ribs: Coat both sides of the ribs with kosher salt and the rub. Cut the ribs into manaeable pieces (3-4 bones in each).
  4. Pressure cook the ribs: Pour the ½ cup of water into the pressure cooker pot. Place the ribs around the pressure cooker, bone side down. Pressure cook on high for 30 minutes, then naturally release the pressure for 15 minutes.
  5. Grill the ribs: Brush the ribs with the barbecue sauce and place or a hot BBQ, bone side down, or on an oven tray under the broiler. Grill until the sauce starts to bubble (about 5 minutes).
  6. Remove the ribs from the heat and coat with another layer of sauce before serving.
