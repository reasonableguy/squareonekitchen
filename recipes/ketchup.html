Ingredients

  • 175ml tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup light corn syrup
  • 1/2 cup white vinegar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon table salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon garlic powder

Method

  1. Place all the ingredients in a medium saucepan on medium heat.
  2. Whisk until combined.
  3. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Add the cheese to the mortar and slowly drizzle in olive oil while working it into the pesto with the pestle. Adjust the amount of olive oil to get your desired consistency.
  5. Remove from heat and cover. Allow to cool to room temperature.
  6. Keep it in a container in the fridge until you need it.
