Cook time:

Total time:

Ingredients

  • 150g dried macaroni elbows
  • 50g butter
  • 200g cheese
  • salt & pepper to taste

Method

  1. Put a medium-seized pot of salted water on the stove to boil.
  2. Once your water is boiling, add the macaroni elbows.
  3. Cook until the pasta is al dente.
  4. When your pasta is cooked sufficiently, drin it through a colander.
  5. Let the pasta sit (don't rinse it, we don't want to cool it down).
  6. Remove about 2/3 of the leaves from the basil and cut into ribbons.
  7. Add the butter to the pot you've just used and melt the butter over a medium-high heat.
  8. If you want to add extras like bacon or onion, you could do that now.
  9. Add the pasta to the melted butter and stir to coat.
  10. Quick release the pressure and open the lid.
  11. Add the cheese and stir virorously until you see cheesy strands starting to form.
  12. Season to taste with salt and pepper (or hot sauce).
  13. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  14. Serve and eat straight away.
