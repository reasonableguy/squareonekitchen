Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 300g "00" flour

Method

  1. Add flour and eggs to a food processor.
  2. Blend briefly until it resembles large couscous.
  3. Squeeze the dough into a ball and wrap in cling film.
  4. Leave at room temperature for 2-4 hours.
  5. Divide the dough into ~100g portions.
  6. Flatten the first ball with the heel of your hand and guide it through the pasta machine on setting 1.
  7. Fold the ends of the length of dough so that they meet in the middle.
  8. Pass the folded dough through again (still on setting 1) rotated 90Â° from your original pass.
  9. Fold the ends in again and pass through on setting 1 (repeat this a few times if you wish).
  10. Pass the dough through at setting 2 (without folding), working all the way up to setting 6 for noodles or lasagne, or setting 7 for ravioli etc.
  11. Buon apetito.
