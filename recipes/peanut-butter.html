Ingredients

  • 2 cups roasted, salted peanuts
  • 1 tablespoon peanut oil (optional)

Method

  1. Place the nuts and oil in a food processor.
  2. If you want chunky peanut butter, reserve 1/4 cup of peanuts to add later.
  3. Blend for around 2 minutes.
  4. Check consistency and blend in 15 second bursts until desired consistency is reached.
