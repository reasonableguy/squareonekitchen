Ingredients

  • 2 litres milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons white vinegar

Method

  1. Prepare a collander over a bowl and line with 2-3 layers of cheesecloth.
  2. Pour the milk and salt into a large pot.
  3. Heat over a medium-low heat (stirring occasionally) until you reach 185Â°F (15-20 mins).
  4. Turn heat down to low and add the vinegar.
  5. Agitate for 2 minutes. Curds will start to form and separate for the yellowish whey.
  6. Remove pot from the heat. Cover and let sit for 20 minutes.
  7. Carefully scoop your curds into the cheesecloth.
  8. Let rest for 3-5 minutes (longer if you want drier ricotta).
  9. Use immeadiately or store in the fridge.
