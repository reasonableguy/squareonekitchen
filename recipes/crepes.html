Cook time:

Total time:

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup cold milk
  • 1 cup cold water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 & 1/2 cups flour
  • 60g melted butter, plus more for brushing on pan

Method

  1. Melt 60g of butter in a small pan and set aside.
  2. In a blender or with a whisk, mix the eggs, milk, and water until smooth. Gradually add the salt and flour, then blend in the melted butter.
  3. Refrigerate the batter for at least 2 hours, or overnight.
  4. Heat a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and brush with melted butter.
  5. Pour 2 to 3 tablespoons of batter into the center of the pan and tilt the pan to cover the bottom evenly. Cook for about 1 minute, or until browned on the bottom, then flip and cook briefly on the other side.
  6. Cool the crêpes on a rack or plate. Serve as desired.
