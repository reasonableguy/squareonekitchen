Ingredients

  • 2 liters of milk
  • 1 TBSP to 1/4 cup yogurt

Method

  1. Add the milk to your instant pot. Close the lid but leave the steam vent open.
  2. Press "Yoghurt" until the readout says "boil".
  3. This will take about half an hour. Once it has finished, remove the lid. Add all the ground beef. Break it up with a wooden spoon. Reduce the heat to medium and cook until all the liquid has evaporated, stirring occasionally.
  4. Roughly chop carrots, onions and celery. Add to the processor and blitz to roughly the same size as the beef.
  5. Add veggies to the pot. Cook for 15 to 20 minutes, or until the vegetables start to soften, stirring regularly.
  6. Add the tomato pur√©e and tinned tomatoes. Fill each of the tins with water and tip into the pan.
  7. Add fish sauce, soy sauce and cocoa.
  8. Stir well and reduce the heat to low. Leave to simmer around an hour, or until thickened and reduced, stirring occasionally.
  9. Season to taste. Blitz in batches.
