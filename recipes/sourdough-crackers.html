Ingredients

  • 100g sourdough starter
  • 70g - 80g Wholemeal Flour
  • 25g olive oil
  • 3g herbs/spices
  • 1/2 tsp salt (plus more for topping)

Method

  1. Blend all of the ingredients in a bowl. The dough should be nice and soft.
  2. Knead a few times and then cover and let sit overnight.
  3. In the morning, preheat the oven to 350Â°F. Roll the dough until it is nice and thin and cut to size on an oven tray.
  4. Top with olive oil and salt.
  5. Bake for about 15 minutes (depending on the thinness of your crackers) until golden brown.
  6. Remove from the oven and let cool.
