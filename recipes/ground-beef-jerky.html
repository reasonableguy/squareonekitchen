  • 2 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoon worcestershire sauce

  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ginger powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon coriander
