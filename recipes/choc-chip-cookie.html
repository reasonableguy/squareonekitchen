Ingredients

  • 1 & 2/3 cups of all purpose flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 egg
  • 1 & 3/4 cup of chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F
  2. Beat butter, sugars and vanilla until light and fluffy.
  3. Add the egg and continue to combine.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking soda and salt.
  5. Add the flour mix to the sugar mixture and beat on low until combined.
  6. Turn the mixer off and stir in the chocolate chips and nuts (if using).
  7. Scoop tablesppon-sized balls onto an oven tray with a couple of inches between the dough balls.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes (until golden).
  9. Remove from oven and allow to cool on the sheet for a minute. Then move them to a wire rack to finish cooling.
