Cook time:

Total time:

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp Gelatine
  • 1/2 Cup Water
  • 2 Cups Sugar
  • 1 Cup Water
  • 1 tsp extract (Vanilla essence, Rose Water etc)
  • A few drops of coloring (optional)

Method

  1. Combine gelatine and 1/2 cup of water in a heat-proof bowl. Leave for 10 mins to absorb.
  2. While the gelatine does its thing, heat sugar and 1 cup of water over a medium-high heat, stirring until the sugar has completely dissolved.
  3. Melt the thickened gelatine over the syrup (double boiler/bain marie style).
  4. When the gelatine is melted, add it to the syrup. Boil constantly for 15 mins. (Keep an eye on it. It boils over easily.)
  5. Remove from heat and allow to cool until luke warm (about 15mins).
  6. When cooled, beat in a stand-mixer with a whisk attachemnt until thick (3-5 minutes).
  7. Add flavor and color (if using) and beat to combine.
  8. Wet an dish with water and pour the marshmallow mixture in.
  9. Chill until set (about 15 mins).
  10. Sprinkle a chopping board with powdered sugar. Using a spatula, ease the marshmallow out (If it's stuck, try warming the pan in a larger pan of hot wtare for 30 seconds).
  11. Cut the marshmallow into cubes. Be sure to coat all the sides with powdered sugar. (You may like to coat your knife with oil or powdered sugar to prevent sticking.)
  12. Enjoy!!
